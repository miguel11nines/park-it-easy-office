Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Park it easy office ("the App") is a parking management application designed for office environments. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information when you create an account and use the service:

  • Authentication data: Email address and name, used to identify your account.
  • Booking data: Parking spot reservations you create, including dates and assigned spots.

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Payment or financial information
  • Data from children under 13 years of age

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and manage your account
  • Allow you to book and manage parking spots
  • Display usage statistics within the App

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encrypted data storage and secure authentication. Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Row-level security policies ensure users can only access data they are authorized to see.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting. See Supabase Privacy Policy.

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
